Biography

I’m a fourth-year PhD student from Insitute of Computational Linguistics (ICL) at Peking University and working on Natural Language Processing under the supervision of Prof. Houfeng Wang. My interests mainly lie in deep learning and sentiment analysis including Aspect-level/Document-level Sentiment Analysis.
